Special Characters and Symbols

In HTML, some characters are reserved and have special meanings. To display these characters, you need to use HTML entities.

  • &copy; - ©: Represents the copyright symbol.
  • &lt; - <: Represents the less than sign.
  • &gt; - >: Represents the greater than sign.
  • &amp; - &: Represents the ampersand symbol.
  • &euro; - €: Represents the euro currency symbol.
  • &trade; - ™: Represents the trademark symbol.
